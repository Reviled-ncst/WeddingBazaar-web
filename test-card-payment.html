  • ❌ Incorrect payment_method_allowed format (['card', 'maya', 'gcash'])
  • ❌ Insufficient error logging made debugging difficult

Fix Applied:

  • ✅ Removed problematic statement_descriptor field
  • ✅ Simplified payment_method_allowed to just ['card']
  • ✅ Added comprehensive logging for request/response debugging
